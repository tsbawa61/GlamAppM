Privacy Policy

GlamTrack Salon Manager

Last updated: June 2025

GlamTrack ("we", "us", or "our") is a salon management platform designed for salon owners, managers, and staff. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using the GlamTrack application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use GlamTrack:

  • Account information: Name, email address, mobile number, and role (Owner, Manager, Staff, Customer).
  • Salon data: Service menus, package definitions, customer visit records, and payment information entered by salon staff.
  • Usage data: Login timestamps and session activity for security and audit purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and manage user accounts securely.
  • To display salon-specific data to authorised users only.
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. OTP codes for administrator access).
  • To provide usage statistics and dashboard insights to salon owners.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is isolated per salon account using a unique owner identifier. No data is shared between different salon accounts.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase: For secure cloud data storage and authentication.
  • EmailJS: Used only to send OTP security codes to system administrators.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or regulation.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your salon account is active. If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact us at the address below and we will process your request within 30 days.

6. Security

Access to GlamTrack is protected by role-based authentication and password verification. We implement industry-standard practices to protect data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (Firebase security rules).

7. Children's Privacy

GlamTrack is a business tool intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

9. Cookies

GlamTrack does not use tracking cookies. The application uses browser cache storage (via Service Worker) only to enable offline loading of the app shell — no personal data is stored in the cache.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of GlamTrack after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
